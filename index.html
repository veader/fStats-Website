f/stats

flickr stats for iPhone

Access to your flickr stats while on the go.




Features

View stats for the last 30 days and an all-time view
NEW Graph view shows daily trends
Take a closer look with the detail view


flickr pro account required



Day View

A quick look into which photos are gaining attention. Total view, favorite, and comment counts available for each photo. Access to more detailed information, including referring domains, is just a tap away.



Graph View

NEW
Introducing graph view, available when the device is rotated to landscape orientation. Graph view allows you to see trends in daily total views. Slide or tap a day in the graph to see the total views.



Detail View

See more detailed information for a photo, such as:

  •   Referring domains
  •   Favorite counts
  •   Comment counts
